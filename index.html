Information for Visa, Travel, Lodging, and Special Needs:

Visa-free policies: Citizens from certain countries do not need a visa to enter China (for a 30 days). Please refer to the following links for details:

  • 30 days Visa-free Policy Q&A-News

If you need a Chinese visa, here is the website for application. For us to prepare a visa acceptance letter for you along with the application, please contact US at icm@sustech.edu.cn

The SUSTech campus can be reached from the Shenzhen Baoan International Airport (40m taxi) or Shenzhen North Railway Station (10m taxi). Hong Kong is close to Shenzhen (1h shuttle) and has more international flight options. There is no need for a separate visa for Hong Kong.
